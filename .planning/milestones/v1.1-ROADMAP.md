## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- Use a stable structured diagnostics schema before broadening validation coverage, so later checks can reuse immutable codes and payload fields.
- Route standalone validation and render preflight through the same environment validator, so users get one truthful readiness contract.
- Test onboarding through the real `create_*()` helper path rather than template-only smoke tests, so first-run success claims stay honest.
- Back performance claims with committed v1.0/current baselines, then close the milestone only after supported-environment runtime evidence passes.

**Issues Resolved:**

- Validation failures now emit deterministic structured diagnostics instead of relying on brittle prose-only assertions.
- Users can check Quarto, Typst, version-floor, and extension readiness before render starts.
- Scaffolded working paper, article, and policy brief starters were proven to validate and render successfully on a supported setup.
- Benchmark-backed optimization claims are now audit-evidenced with no-backslide and semantic-parity coverage.
- Milestone audit traceability gaps were closed across summaries, verification files, validation files, and requirements bookkeeping.

**Issues Deferred:**

- No additional closeout blockers remain. Future validation, onboarding, and performance ideas stay deferred until the next milestone is defined.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**

- No new technical-debt items were accepted at milestone close.
